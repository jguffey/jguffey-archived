Always Learning

Always learning, that's my motto. When I'm not busy working, I'm usually hunched over a computer, book, or coffee table.

I'm interested in things like architecture, graphic design, fashion, ...coffee, but I know what I'm most comfortable doing. I love building. I like designing systems, from planning to implementation, I enjoy creating a solid foundation for a successful product or team. My favorite part of any project is that first line of JavaScript, or cloning a new copy of a framework; that first syncdb.

I like to build things because I learn from doing, and as I mentioned, I love learning. With that first line of code, I'm redefining my approach, trying to find a new faster way of doing things. With every project I try to do at least one thing that is new and challenging.

Besides that, I'm a founding partner and Developer at Collective Ray. I co-run a design team with my best friends Brian, Agustin, and Jeff. Together we design for enterprise companies, start ups, and even ourselves. When I'm not writing plans, I'm creating prototypes or new interfaces using HTML, JavaScript, or Python. When I'm doing none of the above I'm usually taking a bike ride or hanging out with my pup.
